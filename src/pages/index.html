What is the Developer Circles Vietnam Innovation Challenge?”

To upskill Vietnamese student developers and provide them with workplace readiness skills, Facebook is partnering with CoderSchool to launch Developer Circles Vietnam Innovation Challenge - a 18-month training and competition program across Ho Chi Minh City, Hanoi, and Danang focusing 3 tracks: mobile development, product management and data science.

FORMAT

  • A 06 months training and competition program in Ho Chi Minh, Hanoi and DaNang
  • We will select 800 of the most promising candidates from Ho Chi Minh (300); Hanoi (300) and Danang (200) to apply one of three training tracks: Software Engineer; Product Management or Data Science. These candidates will each go through a comprehensive program, tailored to their track , before forming teams and create products to solve problem statements from Partner companies.

Training Details

  • Training program in each cities will be devided into 2 batches. 150 students for each batch which feature the same curriculum and activities. (Developers are trained in batches for logistical reasons only).
  • Each batch will last for 10 weeks. Every theory class will meet twice a week. Each class is two hours long and there will be at least 01 hour a week for mentorship from companies’ mentors.
  • Training schedule:

    • o Time:

    • For theory class: 07pm-09pm. Every Monday and Wednesday
    • At least 01 hour on every Friday for mentoring

    o Venue:

    • Ho Chi Minh: CoderSchool – 12 Ton Dan, Ward 13, District 4.
    • Hanoi & Danang: To be informed.
  • Class will be offered in Vietnamese, with occasional English guest speakers.
