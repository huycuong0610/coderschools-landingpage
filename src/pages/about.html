--- title: About the Challenge ---

What is the Developer Circles Vietnam Innovation Challenge?

To upskill Vietnamese student developers and provide them with workplace readiness skills, Facebook is partnering with CoderSchool to launch Developer Circles Vietnam Innovation Challenge - a 18-month training and competition program across Ho Chi Minh City, Hanoi, and Danang focusing 3 tracks: mobile development, product management and data science.

We aim to train students and fresh graduates with technical skills, team work, presentation and problem solving skills to address prominent problems facing local businesses, corporates, and communities in Vietnam.

Target Audience: 3 rd - 4 th year and fresh graduate Vietnamese local students from universities in Hanoi, Ho Chi Minh and Danang who are creative, driven, entrepreneurial and demonstrate a keen interest in using technology to solve problems.

Our Partner Companies

What they will provide:

  • Problem statements for student to work on
  • Provide mentorship: Mentors from these companies who are CEO, CTO, Project Managers, Technical Lead and Senior Software Engineer will spend at least 1 hour a week to help and coach students during the training time. (Thoery hours will be taught by CoderSchool’s experienced instructors)
  • Share anonymized data or training materials that the students can use for their projects
  • Oﬀer job and/or internship opportunities for exellent students or teams.
  • Sponsor valueable prizes for winning teams.
